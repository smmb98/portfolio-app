Final Year Project

Device-Free Breath rate Detector

2020 – 2021
Project Details:

Using ESP32s, our four-person team created a prototype system for measuring respiration rate from WIFI signals. It was a research experiment in which we attempted to predict a person's respiration rate solely based on WIFI signals. The WIFI signal was sent through the subject's lungs, and the difference in signal was processed to detect the movement of the lungs, resulting in the determination of out-breath rate.

It was previously accomplished using highly expensive equipment and intricate systems, but we replicated the entire project using very inexpensive and simple components to obtain the intended outcome.
